# InBiot Virus Resistance Indicator

**Source:** https://www.inbiot.es/wikinbiot/virus-spread-resistance-indicator

0–100 score for environmental conditions that affect airborne virus survival and transmission. Factors: **RH** (40–60% optimal), **temperature**, **air quality / ventilation**.

| Score | Level | Description |
|-------|-------|--------------|
| 80–100 | High | Conditions significantly reduce transmission risk |
| 60–79 | Good | Favorable for reducing spread |
| 40–59 | Moderate | Some protective factors present |
| 20–39 | Low | Conditions may favor survival/transmission |
| 0–19 | Very low | Conditions may enhance transmission |

**Improve:** Keep RH 40–60%; ensure adequate ventilation (low CO₂); monitor temperature; improve filtration; increase outdoor air when feasible. Relevant in offices, schools, healthcare.
